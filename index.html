Workspace Setup

Dedicated space. Not your bed. Separate work from rest.
Good chair and desk. Back pain kills productivity.
External monitor. Game changer for focus.
Good lighting. Natural light best. Avoid eye strain.
Minimize distractions. Noise-canceling headphones help.

Daily Routine

Get dressed. Pajamas = lazy mode. Clothes signal work.
Start and end at set times. Boundaries prevent burnout.
Morning routine. Don't roll out of bed into meetings.
Commute replacement. Walk before/after work. Transition ritual.
Take real lunch. Step away from screen. Eat elsewhere.

Productivity

Time blocking. Deep work in AM, meetings in PM.
Over-communicate. Remote = no casual visibility. Update often.
Use async when possible. Not every question needs a meeting.
Document everything. Written > spoken for remote teams.

Health & Wellbeing

Move regularly. Set hourly reminders to stand/stretch.
Get outside daily. Sunlight and fresh air matter.
Social connection. Video calls with colleagues. Coffee chats.
Clear end to workday. Shut laptop. Leave "office."
